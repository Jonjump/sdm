SDM : Spiky Donor Management

1. donations

2. benefits

3. reports
Donations are recorded by the payment provider. You load the information they give you into SDM, and manage ALL the donations in one place.

Benefits are the different levels you set for donors - "£10 per month for 6 months", or "a single donation of at least £50 in the last six months"

Summary data for number and amount of donations is provided in a spreadsheet.

What's the point?

Freedom: Use multiple payment platforms to receive donations
Trust: Fulfil your promises by delivering the benefits you promised to each donor
Efficiency: Reporting so you can see what works, and what doesn't
